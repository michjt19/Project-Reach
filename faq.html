Frequently Asked Questions

What is Reach?

Reach is a peer support project connecting people who just need someone to talk to. We're not crisis counselors or therapists — just humans who care.

Do volunteers need prior training?

No prior experience is required. We'll guide you through our Code of Conduct and best practices.

Is this a crisis service?

No. If you’re in crisis or feeling unsafe, we urge you to call 988 or 911.
